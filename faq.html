Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase an item?
Simply browse our categories, click on a product to view details, and contact the seller directly if you're interested!
How can I sell my clothes?
Click on the "Upload" link in the navbar, sign in to your account, and follow the steps to list your item.
Is there a return policy?
Each seller may have their own policy, but you can view our general Refund Policy for more details.
How does style matching work?
Our AI analyzes your preferences (set during registration or in your profile) and matches them with available products to give you personalized recommendations.
